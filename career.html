R & D Microbiologist (Preferably Male)

Qualification:

M.Sc. (Microbiology /Biotechnology/Life Sciences)

Preference for candidates who have Qualified National/State level competitive examinations such as CSIR/UGC/ICAR/DBT - NET or GATE-Life sciences/BET and having valid certifications

Experience:

Minimum 2 year of work experience in Microbiology/Industrial microbiology.

Job Description:
  • Isolation of pure cultures (bacteria/algae/fungi) from various ecological sources.
  • Examine physiological, morphological and cultural characteristics using microbiological tools and other biochemical parameters.
  • Maintenance and preservation of different cultures
  • Basic knowledge of fermentation including commonly used down streaming procedures and willingness to work in shifts (Including Night shift) as and when required. Batch trails at 20–100L fermentation scale.
  • Collecting, preparing and testing samples and analysis.
  • Scientific calculations and interpretation of results.
  • Studying the scientific literature and making a product development plan.
  • Laboratory stock and resources management.
  • A Good team worker and should coordinate with peers and colleagues for completing tasks in time bound manner
Complementary Skill Set required
  • Proficiency in MS Excel, word and power point
  • Scientific writing and presentation.
Renumeration:

Rs25K/month during probation period of 1 year. Candidate would be offered a position in the company after successful completion of probation subject to the performance during probation period. Candidates would also be offered stock options in US based Avisa Myko Inc after completion of probation.

About Avisa Myko Inc /Avisa Biotech

Avisa Myko https://www.avisamyko.com is a San Francisco based synthetic biology startup. The company operates a Research and Developmental facility at RGCB -Bionest Incubation centre, KINFRA Hi-Tech Park, Kalamassery , Kochi . Avisa Myko is funded by silicon valley’s premier Biotech accelerator IndieBio Accelerator, Deeptech fund SOSV and another San Francisco based VC’s. It has been promoted by a group of Scientists and technocrats, with over 100 man-years of experience in microbial technology and its commercialisation. Algal and Fungal biotechnology is the area of expertise for Avisa and it seeks to harness the enormous microbial biodiversity of India in finding solutions for the Nutrition, Nutraceuticals and Pharmaceuticals sector The company is in the process of commercializing different technologies to produce Melanin, Omega 3, Myko proteins and prebiotics and is seeking scientific personnel to carry out R & D and application development work.
