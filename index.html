About Us

Avisa seeks to harness the enormous microbial biodiversity of India in finding solutions for the Nutrition, Pharmaceutical, Industrial and Environment sectors.AVISA has been promoted by a group of Scientists and Entrepreneurs, with over 100 man-years of experience in microbial technology and its commercialisation. Our R&D unit is the leading producer of best soluble melanin which is produced from a natural source. We are also working in the scale up production of algal based DHA. Currently we are also working on the commercial production of EPS, FOS, MOS, Phycocyanin, Phycoreythrin.
