About Us

Avisa seeks to harness the enormous microbial biodiversity of India in finding solutions for the Nutrition, Pharmaceutical, Industrial and Environment sectors.AVISA has been promoted by a group of Scientists and Entrepreneurs, with over 100 man-years of experience in microbial technology and its commercialisation. We have developed and commercialised the technology to Produce Soluble melanin through our sister concern Avisa Myko Inc. DHA omega 3 technology from Algae is developed and ready for commercialization. We are looking for partnerships, licensing opportunities for our DHA omega 3 technology . Currently we are also working on ARA (Omega 6 ) and EPA fatty acid technology and on Prebiotics such as FOS, MOS and XOS.Currently we are also working on the commercial production of EPS, FOS, MOS, Phycocyanin, Phycoreythrin.
